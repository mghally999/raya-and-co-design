About Us

A house built on quiet hands.

Rayaa & Co was founded in 2024 around a single belief: that the most beautiful things in fashion are still made by hand.

Our about us sits in a quiet street in Dubai. There is no machinery on the floor — only embroiderers, a master cutter, two finishers, and the constant soft sound of a needle pulling thread through silk. Each piece passes through six pairs of hands before it leaves us. Nothing is rushed.

We work in small editions because what we make is not built for one season. A Rayaa piece is meant to be passed on — from a mother to a daughter, from one occasion to a lifetime of them.

The process

From thread to finished piece, every step is hand-held.

  1. 01

    Pattern & Cut

    Each silhouette is drafted by our master cutter from raw rolls of silk and crepe.

  2. 02

    Embroidery & Mirror

    Hand-set mirrors, bugle beads, and silk-thread embroidery worked across days.

  3. 03

    Finish & Inspection

    Linings, hand-rolled hems, hidden seams. Final inspection before the piece is numbered.
